One night I placed post-it notes around the office kitchen. When viewed from a particular location, you can see that they form a pattern (the Gamebryo logo). That logo is a trade mark of the Gamebryo game engine.

Many people have done similar things before me.

For example, the artist Felice Varini has been doing art based on 2D image projection into the world for decades.
