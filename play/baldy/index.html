Mt Baldy (Mt San Antonio)

Paul, Baback, and I climbed up to the summit of Mt Baldy.  
 

Yep, here we are, ready for several hours of hiking up 2000 feet. Notice that we aren't that prepared. It turned out to be quite windy and cold. ;) We cowered in the rocks on the way up, to rest sheltered from the wind.
 

The summit, at 10 thousand feet, left us short on breath, but had quite a view!! Definitely worth while. This picture is thanks to a kind stranger with a camera. Yep, we're above the clouds.
